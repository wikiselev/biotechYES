Our Services



Screening yourself for cancer has never been easier and faster!

To get tested follow our three simple steps:





1. Ask your local GP to be screened for cancer with the MeDNA test



2. Go through a simple blood test



3. Get results from your GP in 1 day

Method



Our test detects differences in the DNA methylation


Differences in methylation occur very early on in any cancer.


We are able to diagnose any type of cancer before a tumor appears.
